Info for Restaurants

Chekpleez is a notification system that sends service requests from your customer's phones to your team's smart watches.

Chekpleez helps you to compete with today's fast casual restaurant by empowering your customers be in control.

Benefits

Increase revenue

Today's customers are drawn to places that can best serve their needs quickly.

Increase turn

Get your customers in and out when you're busy in much less time than you do today.

Happiness

Make your customers happy wich a helpful tool that doesn't affect their dining experience

More data

Get critical data on how your team is performing and ratings/comments from your customers

Easy to use

No system integration and fits in naturally with your existing restaurant operations

Embed in app

Power your branded mobile app with al the ChekPleez features using the Mobile Kit
